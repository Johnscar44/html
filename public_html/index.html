Break Down and Budget

Hello, and welcome to a Gaming PC building guide. Throught this website you too can buid the gaming PC of your dreams. With this very indept break down you'll learn what parts are best for what you needs and budget. Its important to make a list of all parts needed so you dont have to go back later.

  1. MotherBoard
  2. Processor
  3. RAM
  4. Graphics Card
  5. Storage
  6. cooling
  7. Power Supply
  8. Case

Once you've got a list of everything you need then you can choose what parts you want to spend the most money on. This can be tricky for people new to building a pc. You want to keep upgrade abilty so the parts that'll you spend the most money on are going to be the newest out. That makes them more expensive. For example and SSD doesnt depend on other parts to proform its best and cant be upgraded itself. This make its a good part to get for cheep so you cant spend more money on a Processor.

Part priority budget
Processor highest Under $500
Graphics Card high Under $1000
Power Supply high Under $1500

Speaking of a processor, this is not something to cheep out on even though you might find something older and cheeper but it works. Once its time to upgrade your socket on the motherboard wont suport newer processors so you have to buy a new one and newer motherboards wont support ddr3 RAM so RAM has to be replaced. If you spent more money in the begining upgrading later becomes cheeper.
